Our story

We manufacture and supply refined petroleum, LNG and petrochemical products in a reliable, efficient, safe and environmentally responsible manner

The Kuwait Integrated Petroleum Industries Company (KIPIC) is a newly established integrated downstream Kuwait Petroleum Corporation (KPC) subsidiary responsible for operating and managing refining, Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) and petrochemicals activities of the Al-Zour complex. KIPIC has made significant strides in setting the right foundation for the new organization. In particular the key achievements over the past year (July 2016 to date) include:

  • Establishment of the KIPIC Legal Entity
  • Appointment of the Board and Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
  • Approval of the Immediate Organization structure by the Board and appointment of the Deputy CEOs (DCEOs) and Managers
  • Approval of KIPIC’s Strategic Goals, Vision and Mission in line with KPC 2040 Downstream Strategic Directions and KIPIC’s Mandate
  • Finalization of KIPIC’s brand identity and guidelines
  • Finalization of the KIPIC Go Live Roadmap and initiation of implementation efforts to launch the company in early 2017
  • KIPIC was launched on May 1st after recruiting most essential staff and onboarding them, and transferring all of the key contracts from its
  • sister companies

KIPIC’s strategic objectives:

  1. Maximize value by optimizing the integration between refining, LNG and petrochemicals
  2. Achieve operational excellence in efficiency, reliability, health, safety and environmental protection
  3. Develop world-class in-house capabilities in operating downstream integrated assets
  4. Manufacture downstream products for domestic and international markets
  5. Catalyze the involvement of Kuwait’s private sector in the downstream petroleum industry
  6. Contribute to diversifying KPC's revenue streams

KIPIC Mandate, Mission and Vision

Mandate

“Manage refinery, petrochemical and LNG import operations in the Al-Zour Complex”

Vision

“To be a leader in integrated refining and petrochemical operations and LNG supply that maximizes shareholder value, achieves operational excellence, unlocks the potential of our people and cares for the community”

Mission

“To manufacture refined petroleum and petrochemical products and supply LNG in a reliable, efficient, safe and environmentally responsible manner to meet Kuwait’s energy demand, maximize profitthrough integration, develop a professional and competent workforce, and enable the development of the local economy”
